Goals

There are a lot of RC datasets out there, and its hard to keep them straight. We are specifically interested in Wenas Creek as our fire-impacted watershed. The goals here are:

  1. review what datasets are published, and what they contain
  2. review what datasets are in prep and what they contain and
  3. explore what datasets are most useful.
